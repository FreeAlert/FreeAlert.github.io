Udacity Classroom Onboarding
Onboarding experience to engage students during the period after they purchase the program and before they land on the program home page. The goal is to emphasize important dates of the program, get students excited about the upcoming projects and encourage them to set up study schedule to build good learning habits.
Group Messages (PC, Mobile, TV)
Before, Xbox Messaging only supported 1:1 communication. High value gamers engage with only 1.3 messages per day. In order to increase engagement by providing easier and quicker communication, I designed group messaging and integrated unique Xbox features into this new feature. It was released November 2016 with positive press coverage.
Xbox App Social Guide
Based on usage heatmaps, I created a relayout, identified the four most popular social features, and combined those into a centralized area called the “Social Guide,” complete with notifications, that was located within the Xbox App. This made it dramatically easier for Xbox App users to access their favorite functions.
Xbox One Guide
Xbox One’s Social Guide lets users interact with other gamers without leaving their games. I designed the Friends list, messaging, and party experiences for the Social Guide. Following launch of my features, the entire Social Guide saw an uptick in usage, accounting for 49% of usage relative to the entire Xbox One experience.
